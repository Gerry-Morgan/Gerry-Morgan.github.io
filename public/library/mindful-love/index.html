Mindful Love: Nurturing Mental Health in Relationships

Wellness & Relationships

Mindful Love explores the intersection of mental health and relationships. Gerry Morgan writes with warmth and candour about the challenges of loving well — and being loved — when life gets complicated.

The book offers practical wisdom for nurturing emotional well-being in partnerships, families, and friendships, drawing on both research and lived experience.

A gentle, grounding read for anyone who wants to love more mindfully.
