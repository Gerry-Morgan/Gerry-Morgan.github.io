Canadian Performance Project

Arts & Culture

Canadian Performance Project outlines a bold vision for celebrating and supporting the performing arts across Canada. Drawing on decades of experience as an educator, songwriter, and cultural advocate, Gerry Morgan presents a framework for nurturing homegrown talent.

The book explores how performance — music, theatre, spoken word, and beyond — can strengthen community bonds and express the richness of Canadian identity.

A call to action for artists, educators, and policymakers who believe in the power of the stage.
