Shaping the Future: A Blueprint for Our Country

Policy & Vision

Shaping the Future is Gerry Morgan’s most ambitious non-fiction work — a sweeping proposal for how Canada can navigate the challenges of the 21st century. Drawing on his background in education and technology, Morgan offers concrete ideas for reforming governance, revitalizing the economy, and building a more equitable society.

The book is unapologetically forward-looking, combining big-picture thinking with practical recommendations.

A blueprint for citizens, policymakers, and anyone who believes Canada’s best days are still ahead.
