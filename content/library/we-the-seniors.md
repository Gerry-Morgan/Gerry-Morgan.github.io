*We the Seniors* is a rallying cry for dignity, respect, and better support for older Canadians. Gerry Morgan — a proud senior himself — writes from the inside, blending personal stories with policy analysis.

The book covers healthcare, housing, isolation, financial security, and the untapped wisdom that seniors bring to every community.

**Part manifesto, part memoir — a book that demands we listen to the generation that built this country.**
