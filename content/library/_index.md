# 📚 The Library

Welcome to the collection of my written works. These books represent decades of storytelling, from my personal legacy as a grandfather to the technical and creative history of my songwriting.

**How to Read:** All books are available for free download. If you enjoy the work and would like to support #GeezerRecords and future Canadian stories, a donation of any amount is greatly appreciated.
