*Go With Love* is a tender, reflective work drawn from the emotional landscape of a life richly lived. Gerry Morgan writes about love in all its forms — romantic, familial, platonic, and self-directed.

The book weaves together prose and poetry in a meditation on what it means to let go, move forward, and carry love as both compass and companion.

**For readers who find meaning in the spaces between goodbye and hello.**
