*Life in the Refrigerator* is Gerry Morgan at his most inventive — blending humour, observation, and a dash of the absurd to explore the small dramas of everyday existence.

With a title that invites curiosity, this book delivers on its promise: a fresh perspective on the mundane, the magical, and everything in between.

**A quirky, thought-provoking read for those who like their literature with a side of surprise.**
