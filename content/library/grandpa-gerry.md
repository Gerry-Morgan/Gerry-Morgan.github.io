*Grandpa Gerry* is a heartfelt book written for Gerry Morgan's grandchildren. It captures the stories, lessons, and laughter gathered over a lifetime of teaching, songwriting, and exploring the world.

Part memoir, part love letter, this book is about the things that really matter — family, creativity, and the ordinary moments that become extraordinary when you write them down.

**A gift for the next generation — and for anyone who believes grandparents are the original storytellers.**
