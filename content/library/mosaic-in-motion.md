*Mosaic in Motion* captures the energy and beauty of Canada's cultural diversity. Gerry Morgan examines how the interplay of traditions, languages, and stories creates a living, evolving national identity.

More than a static portrait, this book looks at the forces — immigration, art, education, and community — that keep the Canadian mosaic moving, growing, and surprising.

**A timely and hopeful exploration of what it means to share a country with the world.**
