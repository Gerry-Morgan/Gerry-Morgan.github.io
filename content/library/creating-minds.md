*Creating Minds* is a deep dive into the nature of human creativity. Drawing on the concept of *Homo Faber* — "Man the Maker" — Gerry Morgan explores why we create, how our tools shape our thinking, and what it means to live a life devoted to making things.

The book bridges philosophy, education, and personal experience, offering a rich meditation on the creative impulse that defines our species.

**For thinkers, makers, and anyone who has ever felt the pull to build something from nothing.**
