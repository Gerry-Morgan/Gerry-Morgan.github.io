## Pantokrator

**A Rock Opera by Gerry Morgan**

*Pantokrator* is Gerry Morgan's most ambitious creative work — a full-length rock opera that weaves together narrative storytelling, theatrical structure, and a sweeping musical score.

---

### About the Work

The title *Pantokrator* (Greek: Παντοκράτωρ, "Ruler of All") draws from deep theological and artistic tradition. The rock opera explores themes of power, faith, redemption, and the human condition — set against a musical landscape that moves through rock, folk, orchestral, and experimental passages.

This is not background music. It is a story told in song, meant to be experienced from beginning to end.
