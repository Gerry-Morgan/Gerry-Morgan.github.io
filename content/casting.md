# 🎭 Join the Troupe

We are currently building a core troupe of **professional singers and dancers** to bring the **Canadian Sky** legacy and the **Pantokrator** rock opera to stages across Canada.

## 🌟 The Opportunity
This is a paid contract engagement based in **Calgary, Alberta**. We are seeking high-energy "Triple Threat" performers who want to be part of an original, narrative-driven Canadian work.

## 📥 Submission Instructions
We are currently accepting digital auditions. Please provide the following:

1.  **Performance Sample:** 
    *   **Singers:** An MP3 or MP4 of you performing two contrasting songs (approx. 1 minute each). 
    *   **Dancers:** An MP4 of a recent performance or reel.
2.  **Headshot & Resume:** A current professional photo and a summary of your performance history.
3.  **Contact Info:** Your current phone number and confirmation that you are Calgary-based.

**Submit your materials to:** [hello@gerry-morgan.github.io](mailto:hello@gerry-morgan.github.io)
*Subject Line: AUDITION: [Your Name] - [Singer/Dancer]*
