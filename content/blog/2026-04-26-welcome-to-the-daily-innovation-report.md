This is the **Daily Innovation Report**. The idea is simple: show up every day, notice something, write about it.

Innovation isn't always a breakthrough. Most of the time it's a small observation — a better way to phrase a lyric, a cleaner approach to a software problem, a teaching method that actually lands. The daily practice is what matters.

Some days the report will be about music. Some days about software. Some days about education, theology, philosophy, or just the weather in Canada. The only rule is: write something honest, every day.

Welcome aboard.

— Gerry
